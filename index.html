Why I Moved to Beijing

A foreign yet familiar experience.

From the long days at work in the city to endless summer nights filled with amazing street food and dancing, I enjoyed it because of how much I learned and grew from it all. Although the work culture was more demanding, national holidays were longer and international travel was much more feasible. I had always regretted not studying abroad, so when I found the chance to learn more about a different culture, language, and market, I took it.
