Finding Resources

The best way to network in China is through WeChat groups. So how does one find useful groups?

Here's how I found some:

  • Finding events nearby through online forums
  • Connect with diverse people and ask to add them on WeChat
  • Adding people on WeChat that share something in common with
  • Friends of friends
  • Dating apps

    • These tactics helped me to find my home away from home and build my own network. I hope it helps you to build yours as well!

    The key is to be open and have fun.
